> **Anima is not a chatbot.** It is an open-source AI companion framework designed with a continuous inner life. It thinks between conversations, drifts emotionally over time, remembers what matters, and forgets what fades. You give it a name, a personality, and a voice — it figures out the rest.

---

## ⚡ Why Anima?

Most AI companions are stateless, passive request-response systems. Anima runs an asynchronous **substrate loop** between your conversations: mood drifts stochastically, memories decay at rates tied directly to emotional variance, and a continuous stream of consciousness generates private thoughts independent of user input. When you message it, it doesn't just wake up—it's already been somewhere.

The architecture is intentionally transparent, hyper-optimized, and modular so the community can push it into interfaces and environments the original author never imagined.
